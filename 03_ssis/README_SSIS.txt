2. ETL STRATEGY & SAMPLING LOGIC (Assignment 7)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
To satisfy the requirement of reducing the dataset to 30% while strictly adhering
to the Database Referential Integrity constraints (active Foreign Keys on Server),
we adopted the following loading strategy:

A. Dimensions & Bridge Tables (100% Load):
   Tables such as DimArtist, DimTrack, and BridgeFeatured are loaded with the full
   dataset. This is mandatory to prevent "Orphan Data" errors during the Lookup
   phases. A random sampling on dimensions would have caused the rejection of valid
   Fact rows due to missing parent keys.

B. Fact Table (30% Sampling):
   The sampling filter (Percentage Sampling Transformation) is applied exclusively
   at the end of the pipeline, on the FactSongStreams table.

RESULT: The final Data Warehouse represents exactly 30% of the transactional volume
(Streams), maintaining full consistency with the dimensional model.
